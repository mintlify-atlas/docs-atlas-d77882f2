---
title: "Register and sign in to your Tikera account"
description: "Create a Tikera account with your name and email address, sign in to access the booking flow, and sign out to end your session securely."
---

Tikera requires an account to book cinema tickets. Registration takes under a minute and only asks for your name, email address, and a password. Once signed in, your session persists across page refreshes so you can browse films and complete a booking without having to log in again.

<Warning>
  Booking tickets requires you to be signed in. If you select a movie without an active session, the booking panel will prompt you to log in before you can proceed to seat selection.
</Warning>

## Creating an account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Register page">
    Navigate to the Register page from the site's navigation. The registration form is open to anyone — no invitation or existing account is needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your name">
    Type your full name in the **Name** field. This is the display name associated with your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email address">
    Provide a valid email address in the **Email** field. This becomes your login identifier and must be unique across all Tikera accounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a password">
    Enter a password of at least 8 characters in the **Password** field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your password">
    Re-enter the same password in the **Confirm Password** field. The form validates that both fields match before submitting. If they differ, an error is shown and the request is not sent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the form">
    Click **Register**. If the details are valid and the email is not already in use, your account is created and you are redirected to the Login page with a success message. If the server returns validation errors (for example, a duplicate email address), they are displayed on the form so you can correct them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Signing in

On the Login page, enter your registered email address and password, then click **Login**. After a successful sign-in your session is established and you are redirected to the Movies page, where the booking panel becomes available.

Your session is preserved across browser refreshes. You do not need to log in again unless you explicitly sign out or your session expires.

## Accessing your bookings

Once signed in, you can visit the **My Bookings** page at any time to see all reservations tied to your account. The page shows booking history with screening details, seat assignments, ticket types, prices, and status. Only your own bookings are visible — each account has an isolated view.

## Signing out

Select the **Logout** option from the navigation to end your current session. Logging out clears your credentials from the browser, and you are returned to a signed-out state. You will need to sign in again to make new bookings or view your booking history.
